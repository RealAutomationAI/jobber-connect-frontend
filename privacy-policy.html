Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how William AI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website, Jobber integration pages, and related services ("Service").

By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone number for account linking
  • Business information
  • Jobber OAuth authorization
  • Call metadata and call summaries
  • Addresses, client details, and job information entered during workflows

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Server logs (IP addresses, timestamps, device info)
  • Integration success/failure logs
  • Disconnection events

1.3 Data From Jobber (With Permission)

  • Client, property, and request information
  • Contact details
  • Job and work request information
  • Relevant CRM records

We only access the data required to provide the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Sync calls and leads into Jobber
  • Create clients, properties, and requests
  • Improve accuracy of AI call summaries
  • Authenticate and maintain your Jobber integration
  • Provide support and troubleshoot issues
  • Send webhooks to your automation system (e.g., n8n)

We never sell your data.

3. Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • Your Jobber account (inside your own Jobber organization)
  • Service providers we use for hosting, database, AI, and infrastructure
  • Third-party tools you explicitly connect (e.g., n8n via webhook)

We do not share data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secure token storage
  • Limited access to production systems
  • Automatic Jobber token invalidation on disconnect

No system is 100% secure, but we take security seriously.

5. Data Retention

We retain Jobber tokens and call data only as long as necessary to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or disconnecting Jobber.

Disconnecting from Jobber immediately invalidates all access tokens.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of account data
  • Disconnect integrations at any time
  • Ask questions regarding data handling

Contact us at: team@realautomation.ai

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18 and we do not knowingly collect their information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised version.
